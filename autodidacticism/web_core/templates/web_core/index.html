Autodidacticism

"Autodidacticism or self-education is education without the guidance of masters or institutions. Generally, autodidacts are individuals who choose the subject they will study, their studying material, and the studying rhythm and time." - Wikipedia

A bit pretentious but this site serves two functions: It serves as a repository for my personal educational projects (and supporting documentation) as well as a platform for Data APIs that serve datasets that are either generated by said projects or personal data collection.

Is this website my personal programming portfolio? Honestly, yes. The Applications section of the website functions more as a traditional programming portfolio but I will probably link this entire site in a resume at some point. Autodidacticism serves more as a means of keeping track of the resources that I use for research and software development. I am far more of a back end oriented individual and do not have a real eye for design/front end so expect the look and feel of the site to be VERY basic.
